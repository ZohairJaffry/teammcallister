WELCOME TO TEAM MCALLISTER

Steve and Jenni McAllister are a husband and wife team that have competed at the international level for over 30 years. Having been selected to four FEI World Cup teams and two Nations Cup teams, they know what it takes to be successful at the highest levels of international competition.

Team McAllister works with hunter/jumper riders who aspire to and are committed to competing at the upper levels of show jumping. Custom training programs are designed for each rider based on their individual goals.
